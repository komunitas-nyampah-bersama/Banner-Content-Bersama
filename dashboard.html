Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To integrate Proof of Attention Value (PoAV), the 2BCycle Framework, and circular economy principles into applied social research that transforms waste into opportunity.

Our Vision

To establish a leading, community-driven research hub focused on decentralized technology and environmental action, creating replicable sustainability infrastructures globally.

Community Model & Impact

Our Community Model

Nyampah Bersama operates on an open-scale, distributed membership model, removing technological or financial barriers for participation. Through accessible platforms such as WhatsApp, Glide, blockchain wallets, and open learning resources, we engage hundreds of participantsâ€”from students to micro-entrepreneurs.

Open Structure

Enables organic scaling while preserving local identity and ownership

Distributed Model

No central authority - community-driven decision making

Accessible Platforms

Reach participants through familiar tools like WhatsApp

Our Impact

By integrating real-world actions like waste separation, clean-up drives, and ecological entrepreneurship with digital records and token incentives (NyampahCoin), we are building a scalable and inclusive movement that proves: "People's waste can shape the world's future."

Syarat & Kewajiban Anggota

Syarat Keanggotaan

  • Mengisi formulir pendaftaran anggota
  • Menyetujui Anggaran Dasar/Rumah Tangga (AD/ART)
  • Menyetujui Kode Etik Komunitas
  • Menyetujui Kebijakan Perlindungan Data Pribadi
  • Bersedia mematuhi prinsip non-diskriminasi dan inklusivitas
  • Menghargai hak asasi manusia
  • (Opsional) Memiliki ketertarikan pada isu lingkungan dan ekonomi sirkular

Kewajiban Umum

  • Mematuhi Kode Etik dan semua kebijakan komunitas
  • Menjaga kerahasiaan informasi sensitif
  • Berinteraksi secara profesional dan tanpa diskriminasi
  • Mendukung visi dan misi komunitas dengan integritas
  • Berpartisipasi aktif dalam upaya komunitas
  • Memromosikan nilai-nilai positif, inklusivitas, dan keberlanjutan
  • Melaporkan pelanggaran etika melalui saluran pengaduan
